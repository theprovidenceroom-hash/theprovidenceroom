About
This room holds space

The Providence Room was born from a belief that everyone deserves a private, dignified space to ask their most honest questions — without judgment, without spectacle.

Every reading is approached with genuine care and deep respect for where you are in your journey. Tarot, in this room, is not about prediction. It is about reflection — a mirror held up to your own inner wisdom.

Whether you are at a crossroads, seeking clarity, or simply curious what the cards have to say — you are welcome here, exactly as you are.

⚖ Important notice — please read

Entertainment & Spiritual Purpose: Tarot readings offered by The Providence Room are intended for entertainment, spiritual guidance, and personal reflection purposes only. They do not constitute legal, financial, medical, or psychological advice of any kind.

No Guarantee: The Providence Room makes no claims of predicting the future with certainty. All guidance is based on the reader's interpretation of the cards and is not guaranteed to be accurate. Readings should not be used as the sole basis for any significant life decision.

Professional Advice: If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, legal difficulty, medical emergency, or financial distress, please seek qualified professional help immediately.

Age Restriction: Services are available strictly to individuals aged 18 years and above only.

Privacy & Data: We collect and process personal data in accordance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023. Your data is never sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties. Request deletion at any time: theprovidenceroom@gmail.com.

Refund Policy: Session fees are non-refundable once a reading has commenced. Cancellations more than 24 hours in advance may be rescheduled once at no charge. No-shows are non-refundable.
